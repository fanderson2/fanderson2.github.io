I love to code, compose, improvise and create live electronic music with bespoke interfaces...

I think it's wrong live electronic music is confined to just pressing a play button when performing, or being constrained to just triggering samples. Computers should be more than just a playback tool - they should be an expressive instrument capable of shortening the gap between the mind and creation to better express ourselves sonically and emotionally.

I'm on a mission to design and develop instruments with various technologies to transform our all too often placid relationship with technology into meaningful, two way communication and art.
